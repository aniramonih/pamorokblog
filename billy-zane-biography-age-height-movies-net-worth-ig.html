Early Life and Education

Born on February 24, 1966, Billy Zane is originally named William George Zane Jr. He was born in Chicago, Illinois, the United States of America, under the star sign, Aquarius.  Zane is the son of Thalia and William George Zane Sr. who are both of Greek descent.

Similarly, Billy grew up with his older sister Lisa Zane who is also an actress and singer. Zane belongs to American nationality.

Talking about his education, Zane completed a year abroad at The American School in Switzerland. After that, he graduated from Francis W. Parker School and attended Harand Camp of the Theater Arts in Elkhart Lake, Wisconsin.

Billy Zane: Professional Life

Zane first entered the film industry from the science fiction movie Back to the Future in 1985 and Critters in 1986. Billy transcended as a villain in the thriller movie Dead Calm. He starred alongside Nicole Kidman and Sam Neil in this movie.  Also, the actor appeared in an independent film, Megaville, in 1990. 

Not only on the silver screen, but the actor later appeared on the small screen, making his debut from Twin Peaks in 1991. Zane portrayed the role of John Justice Wheeler in the TV show.

Till now, Billy Zane has played a part in numerous movies and TV shows such as Crime Story(1988), Going Overboard(1989), Memphis Belle(1990), Femme Fatale(1991), Sniper(1993), The Phantom(1996), Zoolander(2001), Samantha Who? (2009)The Scorpion King 3: Battle for Redemption(2012), Mad Dogs(2015-2016), Holmes & Watson(2018), Curfew(2019), and many more.

But undoubtedly, Zane is remembered for his role in the 1997 blockbuster Titanic. In the movie directed by James Cameron, Zane fulfilled the role of rude millionaire Caledon Hockley, fiance of Rose played by Kate Winslet. The American actor portrayed the character so well that he was nominated as Best Villain in MTV Movie Award and SAG Award.

Apart from movies and shows, the talented actor even voiced video games like SSX Tricky(2001). The following year, he gave Kingdom Hearts a voice as a protagonist, Ansem, Seeker of Darkness.
