Who is Jimmy O Yang?

Jimmy O Yang is an American actor, stand-up comedian, and writer who is active in the industry since 2008. O Yang was seen in projects like Silicon Valley (2014), Bernard Tai in Crazy Rich Asians (2018), Dun Meng in Patriots Day (2016), etc.

In 2023, he will be seen in the comedy-action TV series American Born Chinese where he played the role of Ao Guang. Some of the main cast are Michelle Yeoh, Ke Huy Quan, Yeo Yann Yann, Chin Han, and Ben Wang.

He was also featured in Crazy Rich Asians (a romantic-comedy film) alongside other popular actors of this project Constance Wu, Henry Golding, Gemma Chan, Lisa Lu, Awkwafina, and Ken Jeong.

His upcoming screen projects, Interior Chinatown as Willis Wu, Jentry Chau vs. The Underworld, and The Monkey King as Monkey King (voice) are in the post-production stage and will release soon. 

Jimmy O Yang- Age, Parents, Ethnicity, Education

His birthdate is 11 June 1987 and he was born in British Hong Kong as Au-yeung Man-sing. The name of his father is Richard Ouyang (actor) and his mother’s identity is hidden.

Further, Jimmy O Yang’s ancestry is Shanghainese, and has a brother.

As per his academics, he first went to John Burroughs Middle School plus Beverly Hills High School. Then he enrolled at the University of California, San Diego to study economics.
