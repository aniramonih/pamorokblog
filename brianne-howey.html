Brianne Howey is an American actress who made her debut television appearance as Stacey on 90210, a teen drama series. She is known for her roles like playing Kat Rance in the Fox supernatural horror The Exorcist, Georgia Miller in the Netflix dramedy Ginny & Georgia, Shauna Babcock in the Fox thriller The Passage, Scarlet in the British sitcom I Live with Models, and Reagan in The CW superhero drama Batwoman. In addition to that, Howey has also been cast in films such as Horrible Bosses 2, Time Trap, XOXO, Little Bitches, and Viral.
