Michael Irvin Mugshot 2024: Was He Arrested For Sexual Misconduct Allegations?

 Posted on August 15, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  476 words  |  Billy Koelling

After the police confirmed his investigation, fans were curious about Michael Irvin Mugshot.

Irvin has a thing for drama, and he needs to stir something up everywhere he goes. 

Football player turned sports commentator, Michael Irvin spent his whole 12-year tenure as a wide receiver for the Dallas Cowboys in the NFL.

In 2007, they inducted him into the Pro Football Hall of Fame.

Irvin attended Piper High School in Sunrise, Florida, before becoming a football prodigy at St. Thomas Aquinas High School in Fort Lauderdale.

While attending St. Thomas Aquinas High School, the University of Miami approached Irvin to play for the Miami Hurricanes.

Michael Irvin Mugshot in 2024

Well, there is no Michael Irvin mugshot, as no arrests have been made as of yet.

However, the Allen Police Department in Texas has confirmed that he is under investigation. 

The police department accepted that there was evidence of an allegation but declined to provide any information.

They cite the reason because the case is currently in its preliminary stages of investigation.

Levi McCathern, Irvin’s attorney, denied the charges, claiming that Irvin had not engaged in inappropriate or wrongful activity.

McCathern maintained that the undisclosed claim has “absolutely no truth” and that the case, like previous accusations against Irvin, will be insignificant.

Alex Riethmiller, an NFL Media representative, confirmed that he knew the story but did not provide any other information.

Last year, NFL Network and ESPN fired Irvin from his Super Bowl week announcing jobs.

This followed accusations of Irvin inappropriately soliciting a Marriott hotel employee.

Furthermore, McCathern also expressed frustration over the protracted delay in releasing video footage relating to the earlier incident.

He expressed his displeasure, saying,

“I’m mad as hell that they’re hiding this information that is so important to my client’s livelihood.”

McCathern added, “I’m not sure what is on the video. We haven’t seen it. But I believe Michael, at the very least, has the right to see the tape.”

Irvin’s Previous Scandals

Michael’s life has been marred by controversy, and fans don’t look up to him. 

On July 4, 2007, Irvin faced allegations of sexual assault while visiting the Seminole Hard Rock Casino in Hollywood, Florida.

Charges were never made, but a civil complaint was brought against him in 2010.

Irvin launched a $100 million defamation countersuit, but the court dismissed it when the parties settled outside of court in January 2011.

In June 2001, authorities detained Irvin for cocaine abuse, a year after he retired from the NFL.

Irvin and an unrelated woman did not answer the door when the authorities came with a search warrant in a Dallas apartment.

Police then forcibly entered the flat, discovering drugs. Irvin and his partner were arrested, but the accusations against Irvin were eventually dismissed.

He faced a traffic stop in Plano, Texas, for reckless driving on November 25, 2005.
