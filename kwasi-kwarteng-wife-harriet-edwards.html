Who Is Kwasi Kwarteng?

Kwasi Kwarteng is a politician from the United Kingdom, who has been appointed as the Chancellor of the Exchequer by the newly elected Prime Minister of Britain Liz Truss on Tuesday, 6th September 2022. Previously, he was serving as the Secretary of State for Business, Energy, and Industrial Strategy.
