Remembering Suzanne Shepherd: An Icon of Stage and Screen

Suzanne Shepherd, a luminary in the world of acting, directing, and teaching, passed away peacefully on Friday, November 17, 2023, at the age of 89. Her enduring legacy spans decades of theatrical brilliance.

Born in 1928, Suzanne Shepherd made an indelible mark in American cinema and television. Her notable roles in films like Mystic Pizza, Goodfellas, Requiem for a Dream, and A Dirty Shame, along with her portrayal of Mary DeAngelis in the TV series The Sopranos, showcased her exceptional talent.

Beyond her contributions to the screen, Shepherd made history as the first woman to teach Sanford Meisner’s program of acting study at the Neighborhood Playhouse School of the Theatre in New York City. Her impact on aspiring actors and the theatrical community at large was profound.
