Mob Wives: New Blood has been full of drama this season, and a lot of that drama has revolved around newcomer Alicia DiMichele, and her and her incarcerated husband Eddie “Tall Guy” Garafalo. Since Mob Wives: New Blood began fans have been baffled as to why Alicia DiMichele hasn’t left her husband, seeing as how since he went to jail she discovered he had been cheating on her and she is also facing felony charges because of crimes he committed – she claims she is innocent but as we exposed today on CDL that is highly doubtful.

But, according to our exclusive inside source on the Mob Wives, Alicia DiMichele has actually already served her husband Eddie Garafalo with divorce papers! Alicia has remained pretty mum on the status of her and Eddie’s relationship, and just states that she and Eddie are “separated,” when she is asked about her marriage. It makes sense that Alicia has been vague about the status of her marriage, because she doesn’t want to spoil the dramatic season finale of Mob Wives: New Blood.

According to our inside source the season finale of Mob Wives: New Blood will revolve around Alicia DiMichele and her husband Eddie Garafalo’s divorce. Now, we can’t exactly judge Alicia for divorcing her hubby Eddie, he did cheat on her and get her arrested, and get all of her assets frozen. But, we can’t help but bring up the Mob Wives supposed code of loyalty. If Alicia were loyal, shouldn’t she be standing by her husband right now during this time, instead of divorcing him and cutting ties to him to help her own court case? Of course as we also reported exclusively, Alicia cheated on Eddie with Rob LaScala even before she knew Eddie had cheated on her!

Do you blame Alicia DiMichele for divorcing Eddie Garafalo, or would you have done the same thing in her situation?
