Who is Mike Birbiglia?

Mike Birbiglia is a famous American stand up comedian, director, producer, actor, and writer. He wrote, starred, and directed the comedy-drama ‘Don’t Think Twice’ in 2016. Also, he has made his appearances in the films like ‘Your Sister’s Sister’ in 2011, and ‘Trainwreck’ in 2015.

Who are the parents of Mike Birbiglia?

He was born on June 20, 1978, in Shrewsbury, Massachusetts, United States. He is currently 40 years old. His father’s name is Vincent Paul Birbiglia and his mother’s name is Mary Jean. He is the youngest of four children of his parents. One of his brother’s name is Joe Birbiglia.

Mike holds American citizenship but his ethnicity is a mix of  Italian, Irish, some English, German, and Dutch.

Mike Birbiglia: Education, School/College University

He attended St. John’s High School, Shrewsbury, Massachusetts for one year, and graduated from St. Mark’s School, Southborough, Massachusetts in 1996. After that, he attended Georgetown University in Washington, D.C., graduating in 2000. During his college time, he performed as a member of the Georgetown Players Improv Troupe, and also performed at DC Improv in Washington D.C.

Mike Birbiglia: Professional Life and Career

Mike moved to New York in 2000 and in the ‘Late Show with David Letterman’ after two years. He became a storyteller and later worked as a semi-regular contributor to ‘The Moth’ storytelling series in 2003.

He released his first album titled ‘Dog Years’ in 2004 and later released other albums such as ‘My Secret public Album’, ‘Volume 1a’ in 2005, ‘Two Drink Mike’ in 2006, and ‘My Secret public Journal Live’ in 2007.

In 2008, he opened a one-man show ‘Sleepwalk with Me’ and the shoe became a major success. He began his directing career in 2012 from the film version of his show. His film ‘Don’t Think Twice’ was released in 2016 and his comedy special Thank God for Jokes was released on Netflix on February 28, 2017.

Recently, he appeared as Oscar Langstraat in ‘Billions’ and as Howard in ‘Summer Camp Island’ in 2018.

Mike Birbiglia: Awards, Nominations

He was nominated for American Comedy Awards in the category Comedy Special of the Year for ‘Mike Birbiglia: My Girlfriend’s Boyfriend’ in 2014, won Screen Actors Guild Awards in the category Outstanding Performance by an Ensemble in a Comedy Series for ‘Orange Is the New Black’ in 2016, and also won several awards and nomination in his career till date.
