Her support for LGBTQ rights has been fundamental to her career. Her unwavering dedication to supporting LGBTQ people is evident in her work with Keshet’s LGBTQIA Teen Shabbatonim, where she has the privilege of interacting with young visionaries.

Additionally, her involvement with organizations such as Jewish Voice for Peace, known for its inclusive stance on LGBTQ rights and justice, further highlights her tireless commitment to the LGBTQ cause.
