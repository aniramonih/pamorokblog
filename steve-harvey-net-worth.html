Steve Harvey’s Real Estate Investments

Steve’s real estate portfolio is as impressive as his entertainment career. He leases a luxurious 12,000-square-foot mansion in Beverly Park, Los Angeles, for about $1.3 million annually.

Additionally, he owns a 9,000-square-foot residence in Atlanta and a large property in Dallas. In 2020, he acquired a luxurious 35,000-square-foot mansion in Atlanta, previously owned by Tyler Perry, for $15 million.

His strategic real estate investments reflect his financial acumen and taste for luxurious living.

Steve Harvey’s Business Ventures

His business ventures extend beyond television. His entrepreneurial spirit led him to write the bestseller Act Like a Lady, Think Like a Man, which inspired a hit movie. In 2014, he founded Delightful, a dating site, and co-created the show Little Big Shots with Ellen DeGeneres.

His corporation, Steve Harvey Global (SHG), consolidates his interests, including a production company and rights to international versions of Family Feud. SHG also hosts the annual Sand and Soul Festival, blending music, comedy, and audience engagement.

Philanthropic Efforts

Beyond entertainment, he is committed to giving back. His Steve and Marjorie Harvey Foundation focuses on improving the lives of young people through education and outreach. This philanthropy enhances his public image and legacy, blending wealth and goodwill.
