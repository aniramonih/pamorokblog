Introduction to Julie Andrews

 

Julie Andrews is a legendary figure in the entertainment industry, known for her grace, talent, and versatility. Born on October 1, 1935, in Walton-on-Thames, Surrey, England, she has left an indelible mark on audiences worldwide through her acting, singing, and writing.

With a voice like no other and a magnetic stage presence, Andrews captivated hearts from her Broadway debut in “The Boy Friend” in 1954. However, it was her portrayal of Maria in the film “The Sound of Music” in 1965 that truly launched her into global stardom.
