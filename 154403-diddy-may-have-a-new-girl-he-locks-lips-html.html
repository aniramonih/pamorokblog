The death of Kim Porter

In November 2018, P. Diddy was shocked when it was reported that his ex-girlfriend, and the mother of his children, Kim Porter passed away at 47-years-old.

Sean "Diddy" Combs lost his ex-girlfriend and the mother of three of his children, and he mourned her death like all the other close family and friends she had.

The model passed away due to Lobar Pneumonia and was found unresponsive when the Los Angeles County Sheriff's Office responded to the death investigation reported by concerned neighbors.
