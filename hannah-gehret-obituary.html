The harrowing accident

Homan’s truck tipped onto its side as a result of the incident, which sent both cars off the road and into a nearby field.

Homan and his young passenger were not seriously hurt and were able to leave their car on their own.

Tragically, Hannah Gehret got stuck in her Honda and needed mechanical help to get out.

She regrettably passed away at the spot due to her injuries despite the efforts of first responders.

Launching the investigation

The Darke County Sheriff’s Office, Osgood Rescue, Osgood Fire Department, CareFlight, and the Darke County Coroner’s Office were among the agencies who responded quickly to the accident to conduct a comprehensive investigation.

The investigation is still underway, and no new information has been made available to the public.
