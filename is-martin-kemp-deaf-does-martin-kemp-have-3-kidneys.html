Is Martin Kemp deaf? Does Martin Kemp have 3 kidneys?

 Posted on August 25, 2024  |  1 minutes  |  172 words  |  Kelle Repass

Martin Kemp does not have any hearing impairments that are publicly known. However, he has revealed that he has three kidneys.

Martin Kemp made this revelation during an appearance on ITV’s ‘Loose Women’ and joked about stealing the kidney of his older brother and Spandau Ballet bandmate Gary, who only has one such organ.

In June 2020, Martin Kemp, with his son Roman premiered as hosts of the new Sunday breakfast programme for ITV, Martin & Roman’s Sunday Best!

In April 2021, Kemp and his son Roman Kemp began presenting Martin & Roman’s Weekend Best; a spin-off of Martin & Roman’s Sunday Best!

Martin Kemp was announced as the thirteenth celebrity to participate in the summer series of Celebrity Big Brother 2012 and was placed third on 7 September 2012.

From February to April 2016, Martin Kemp appeared as The Childcatcher in the UK and Ireland tour of the musical Chitty Chitty Bang Bang. On 24 March 2021, Kemp and his son Roman were featured in an episode of ITV’s DNA Journey.
