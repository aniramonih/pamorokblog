Jimmy Kimmel is an American television host, writer, comedian, and producer who is best known for being the host and executive producer of the late-night talk show, Jimmy Kimmel Live! He has also served as the host of shows like The Man Show and Win Ben Stein’s Money. Jimmy has appeared in several films and TV shows such as Project X, Monsters vs Aliens, Hellboy II: The Golden Army, The Boss Baby, The Heyday of the Insensitive Bastards, I’m with Her, The Man Show, Robot Chicken, Sesame Street, Shark Tank, The Middle, and many more. During the course of his career, he has been honored with numerous awards such as the Daytime Emmy Award, Producers Guild of America Award, Critics’ Choice Television Award.

They decided to break up in 2008 before deciding to give their relationship another go a couple of months later. They ended their relationship for good in 2009.

They got engaged in August 2012 and got married in July 2013 in a lavish ceremony attended by leading celebs from the industry. Their first child Jane Kimmel was born in July 2014.
