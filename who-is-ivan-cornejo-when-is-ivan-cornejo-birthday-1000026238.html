Who is Ivan Cornejo?

Ivan Cornejo, born in 2004 in Riverside, California, is an American singer-songwriter who delves into the captivating realm of regional Mexican music. What's truly intriguing about Cornejo is the inspiration he draws from his past. It turns out that a breakup during his middle school years became a catalyst for his songwriting journey. Isn't it fascinating how life experiences can shape our creative expression?

Driven by his passion for music, Cornejo took it upon himself to learn the guitar. Armed with determination and the power of online resources, he turned to YouTube tutorials to teach himself how to play this beautiful instrument. Imagine the satisfaction and excitement he must have felt as he strummed his first-ever song on the guitar: "La Bamba" by the legendary Ritchie Valens.

The release of Cornejo's debut album, "Alma Vacia," proved to be a turning point in his career. It climbed to impressive heights, peaking at number two on the US Billboard Regional Mexican Albums chart and number seven on the prestigious Top Latin Albums chart. With 7,000 album-equivalent units sold, the album even reached number 156 on the highly coveted Billboard 200 chart. What's even more remarkable is that "Alma Vacia" held its ground in the top ten of the Regional Mexican Albums chart for an astonishing 35 weeks. Talk about leaving a lasting impression!

Not only did Cornejo's songwriting prowess earn him recognition, but it also propelled him to the top of the Billboard Latin Songwriters chart during the week ending October 30, 2021. The impact of "Alma Vacia" extended beyond charts and accolades, as it was hailed as one of the best albums of 2021 by The New York Times. It's incredible to witness the ripples his music created in the industry.

One of the standout tracks from the album, "Está Dañada," gained significant traction on TikTok in September 2021. The infectious nature of the song propelled it to the 61st spot on the illustrious Billboard Hot 100 chart, making it the second regional Mexican song to achieve such a feat. No wonder Billboard magazine featured Cornejo in their esteemed "Artist on the Rise" column in October 2021. The spotlight was rightfully shining on him.

Continuing his musical journey, Cornejo released his highly anticipated second studio album, "Dañado." The album made an impressive debut, reaching the coveted number-one position on the Regional Mexican Albums chart during the week ending June 18, 2022. It sold 8,000 album-equivalent units and amassed a staggering 11.9 million on-demand streams for its enchanting melodies. Additionally, "Dañado" secured the fourth position on the Top Latin Albums chart and even landed at number 28 on the Billboard 200 chart. This milestone not only solidified Cornejo's status as a rising star but also provided Manzana Records with their first-ever number-one album credit. What a monumental achievement for both the artist and the record label!

In recognition of his exceptional talent, Cornejo received a nomination for New Regional Mexican Artist at the prestigious 2022 Premios Juventud. It's awe-inspiring to witness his trajectory as he continues to captivate audiences with his unique musical style.

Cornejo draws inspiration from acclaimed acts such as T3R Elemento and Grupo Los De La O, shaping his artistic vision. A delightful touch to his songs is the incorporation of guitar solos infused with alternative rock influences. It seems that Cornejo's musical journey intertwines with his upbringing, as he pays homage to a genre that resonated deeply with his brother and mother.
