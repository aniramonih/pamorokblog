Katie Holmes, Suri Cruise Live It Up at the 2015 Kids' Choice Awards

 Posted on August 17, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  241 words  |  Martina Birk

Life of the party! Katie Holmes treated her daughter Suri Cruise to a fun night out at the 2015 Nickelodeon Kids’ Choice Awards, where they rubbed shoulders with the likes of Angelina Jolie, Shiloh and Zahara Jolie-Pitt, Nick Jonas, and Jennifer Lopez.

Suri, 8, looked to be having a blast at the show, which took place at The Forum in Inglewood, Calif. Sporting a multicolored dress and denim vest, Holmes’ mini-me grinned and threw her hands up in delight as orange blimp-shaped confetti rained down on the audience of young, screaming fans.

The Woman in Gold actress, for her part, was the epitome of effortless chic in a floaty printed tank and slim-fit beige pants. She completed the look with hoop earrings and a high ponytail.
