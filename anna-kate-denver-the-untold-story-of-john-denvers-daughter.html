Many people get popular due to their work, and some get due to their relationships. Today we are talking about Anna Kate Denver, known as the daughter of the late singer John Denver. John was one of the most popular acoustic artists in the seventies.

Being the daughter of one of the best-selling artists, Anna has kept a low profile by staying away from the limelight. After her father's demise, John, most of his fans, have been searching for his child, especially Anna. 

Since Anna Kate Deuchendorf is the singer's adopted daughter, people are interested in knowing more about her life. We will know about her personal life in detail below.

Is Anna Kate Denver Married? Who is Anna's Husband?

Yes, Anna Kate Denver has been married for the past couple of years. Anna's enjoying a blissful marital life with her husband, Jaime Hutter of Wanaka, New Zealand. There isn't any information on how and when the pair first met.

Talking about Anna's spouse, Jaime is an Investment Banker. In addition, Hutter is also a philanthropist who has raised money for several charitable causes. 

While Jamie was fundraising for St. John NZ, Coastguard NZ, and Wanaka Search and Rescue Inc, he set a new record for the 24-hour indoor row.

The couple shares one daughter named Daisy Eloise Hutter from their years-long marital life. Kate gave birth to her child, Daisy, on December 21, 2011.

Anna Kate Denver, Adopted Daughter Of John Denver

Anna Kate Denver, aka Anna Kate Deutschendorf, was adopted by the most popular acoustic artist John Denver. Aka Henry John Deutschendorf Jr. John and his first wife, Annie Martell, adopted Kate in 1976. 

But not much information about Anna's biological parents is known. It looks like she was adopted by a local adoption agency. 

Anna grew up in Aspen, Colorado, with her brother, Zachary John Denver. Zachary was also adopted by the Denver family. From various sources, it has been heard that John loved his kids more than anything. 

Talking in an interview, The Musician said, "When I die, Zachary John and Anna Kate's father, boy, that's enough for me to be remembered by."

Anna Kate Denver's Parents' Married Life

John Denver and Annie Martell exchanged vows on June 9, 1967, when John's singing career had just started. After living in Edina, MN, at the beginning of their relationship, the pair moved to Aspen after several years.

When John found fame, their lives turned upside down, leading them to split. In 1982 their divorce was finalized. According to John, their split was due to the work commitment which drove them to go apart. He even said that they were not mature enough to handle his fame in his documentary.

Moreover, after six years of the split with his ex-wife, John tied the knot with Australian actress Cassandra Delaney on August 12, 1988, in an intimate ceremony. However, their marital life didn't last long, they separated in 1991 and finally divorced in 1993. 

Anna Kate Denver's Father, John Denver, died in a Plane Crash.

On October 12, 1997, the most popular singer, John Denver, lost his life in a plane crash. Denver, an experienced pilot, crashed his plane into Monterey Bay near California. 

The accident occurred while he was making a series of touch-and-go landings at the nearby Monterey Peninsula Airport. During his death, the singer was 53 years old, and Anna was 20 years old.

Anna Kate Denver Has Two Siblings: One Brother and Little Sister

John Denver family Anna Kate Denver has two siblings, one brother, Zachary John Denver, born in May 1974, and Jesse Belle Deutschendorf, born in May 1989. 

While Anna and her brother, Zachary, were adopted, Jesse is the biological child of John. The legend and his second wife, Cassandra, gave birth to Jesse.

All three siblings are close to each other. Anna's older brother Zak and younger sister Jesse attended the Hollywood Walk of Fame presentation ceremony for their dad in 2014.
