Meaghan Oppenheimer: Meet the Talented Screenwriter

 Posted on August 11, 2024  |  4 minutes  |  842 words  |  Patria Henriques

Meaghan Oppenheimer is an established screenwriter and producer in Hollywood. Here’s all we know about her. 

When you hear the name Oppenheimer, you automatically think of the famous physicist Robert Oppenheimer, who left a lasting legacy in the world of science and global politics. Is Meaghan Oppenheimer related to him? Keep reading to find out. 

Who Is Meaghan Oppenheimer?

Meaghan Oppenheimer is an American television and movie screenwriter, producer, and former child actress. She was born to parents Reed and Gabriella Oppenheimer on March 28, 1986, in Tulsa, Oklahoma. She grew up in Tulsa with her four siblings and parents, who are educated and have impressive academic credentials. 

Her father, Reed, attended Yale University and bagged a bachelor’s degree in History with an Anthropology minor. Her mother, Gabrielle, has two degrees to her name. She has a history degree from Wheaton College in New York City and an Art History degree from Hunter College in New York City. 

Meaghan herself also has an impressive educational background. She graduated from Holland Hall, a private school in Tulsa, and then moved to New York City to enroll in the prestigious New York University Tisch School of the Arts. Meaghan graduated in 2009 and then relocated to Los Angeles to pursue her career in screenwriting. 

A year after she started screenwriting professionally, Meaghan wrote and starred in “Hot Mess,” a movie about two roommates who became friends with benefits. This breakthrough movie put her on the map and in demand in Hollywood.

She has written several screenplays for many projects thanks to her incredible talent. She has also worked with some of Hollywood’s biggest names, including famous actress Reese Witherspoon and filmmaker Max Joseph.

Meaghan co-wrote the screenplay for the movie “We Are Your Friends” with Max Joseph, which premiered in 2015. That same year, she worked on a TV series titled “Please Don’t Go,” produced by Hollywood hotshot Reese Witherspoon. 

Meaghan Oppenheimer’s Movies and TV Shows

Meaghan started her career in Hollywood when she was only 23 years old. She found success early and has built a strong career that has withstood the test of time. However, many do not know that Meaghan was a child actor in her earlier years. Her most relevant role from her childhood was as a child star in the Disney TV series “Even Stevens.”

It is safe to say that Meaghan has had an incredible run with her career in Hollywood. She has received accolades and features from top magazines such as Forbes and Elle Magazine. Meaghan is expected to try out her hand at her first directing role in an upcoming TV series called “Second Wife,” and fans can’t wait to watch her pull off another part. 

Is Meaghan Oppenheimer Related to Robert Oppenheimer?

No, Meaghan and Robert Oppenheimer, the famous physicist, are not related. The Oppenheimer name is uncommon, and it is a reasonable assumption to think the two are related in one way or the other. 

They have no blood connections or family ties. Their surnames being the same is a coincidence that fans have noticed since Meaghan came into the spotlight. 

Robert, the world-renowned physicist and “father of the atomic bomb,” was born in 1904 and died in 1967, years before Meaghan was born. This further signifies that Meaghan and Robert never met and had nothing in common except for their last names. 

Meaghan Oppenheimer’s Engagement Ring

Meaghan got engaged to her boyfriend of two years and fellow actor, Tom Ellis, in 2017. The screenwriter took to her Instagram page to share the good news with fans on March 27, with a picture of her and the “Lucifer” actor while holding up her gorgeous ring for the world to see. 

Her engagement ring generated a lot of buzz thanks to its stunning beauty and alleged thousand-dollar price. Meaghan’s ring is a beautiful gray diamond engagement ring made by New York-based jeweler Gillian Conroy. 

Ellis reportedly spent over $18,000 on the ring, but the exact price has yet to be confirmed. The couple exchanged vows two years later and married on June 2, 2019. The private event took place at the Grassini Family Winery in Santa Barbara, and the couple remains married to date. 

Meaghan and her husband, Tom Ellis, are still active in the entertainment industry with multiple upcoming projects that fans cannot wait to see.
