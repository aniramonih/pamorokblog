Jaclyn Smith first starred in the iconic 1970's TV series Charlie's Angels, and was considered a sex symbol in Hollywood. Now, almost 50 years later, Smith still looks the same into her 70s. She posted a photo on Instagram with her son, and many commenters said that she looked much younger than him. Read on to see 5 ways Jaclyn Smith stays in shape and the photos that prove they work—and to get beach-ready yourself, don't miss these essential 30 Best-Ever Celebrity Bathing Suit Photos!

Smith broke down her health and wellness secrets to Closer, where she revealed that her most important tip is getting enough sleep every day. "No. 1, sleep is very important," she said. 

In order to maintain her incredible skin and face, Smith has to prioritize her skin care. However, she tells Closer that while having a skin care routine is important, you don't have to put too much pressure on yourself to complete it, and it doesn't have to be too intricate. She says that one of her wellness tips is, "Having a skin-care routine that you don't have to be a slave to."

Smith tells Closer that she avoids certain substances, specifically alcohol and nicotine. "Drinking and smoking — I do neither of those, and smoking is particularly aging."

Smith tells Closer that she always makes time for herself, even with her busy schedule. "Be good to yourself! Every week, or every day, just sit and read a book or soak in the bath."

On her Instagram page, Smith shared a video of her making her go-to healthy smoothie recipe. The smoothie contains ingredients like cucumbers, celery, apples, and lemons. She captioned her video, "It's delicious and gives me a boost of energy!"
