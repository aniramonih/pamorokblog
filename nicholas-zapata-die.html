Who was Nicholas Zapata?

How did Nicholas Zapata die? What was his cause of death?

Sadly, despite efforts to save him, the young man, identified as Nicholas M. Zapata from Normal, Illinois, passed away at 10:59 p.m. on the same night in the emergency room at Carle BroMenn Medical Center. Later, at around 11:47 p.m., deputies from the McLean County Sheriff’s Office and officers from the Bloomington Police Department went to a rural area outside Heyworth, Illinois, and arrested a 16-year-old boy who is suspected of being involved in this incident.
