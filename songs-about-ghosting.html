10 Songs About Ghosting To Get You Through the Pain

 Posted on August 11, 2024  |  6 minutes  |  1077 words  |  Martina Birk

1.Somebody Else by The 1975

“So, you turned around and asked me if I want to get married, and it’s a shame but I can’t say yes to you.”

The opening lines of “Somebody Else” capture the feeling of being left behind after a relationship ends abruptly.

Somebody Else” is a melancholic song by the English pop-rock band, The 1975.

It was released in 2016 as the second single from their second studio album, “I Like It When You Sleep, for You Are So Beautiful yet So Unaware of It.”

The song features lead vocalist Matty Healy’s emotive voice, which perfectly captures the pain of a failed relationship. Its haunting melody and introspective lyrics have made it a fan favorite and a staple in the band’s live shows.

2.Big God by Florence + The Machine

You need a Big God, big enough to hold your love.

Florence Welch’s ethereal voice floats over a haunting melody in “Big God,” a song that deals with the aftermath of being ghosted.

Big God is a hauntingly beautiful song by British indie rock band Florence + The Machine,

It was released in 2018 as a single from their fourth studio album “High as Hope.”

The song features Florence Welch’s ethereal vocals and powerful lyrics that explore the aftermath of being ghosted, emphasizing the need for a big and powerful god to help cope with the pain and heartbreak.

3.Shadow by Britney Spears

This is my time to shine, this is my time to fly.

Britney Spears’ upbeat pop song “Shadow” deals with the aftermath of being ghosted, emphasizing the feelings of confusion and hurt caused by feeling invisible.

Shadow is a song by the American pop singer Britney Spears, released as a single in 2004.

The track was written and produced by Swedish songwriter duo Christian Karlsson and Pontus Winnberg

It was included on the soundtrack of the movie “Catwoman.”

The song is an upbeat pop track that deals with the aftermath of being ghosted, emphasizing the feelings of confusion and hurt caused by feeling invisible.

4.Hello by Adele

”Hello, it’s me. I was wondering if after all these years you’d like to meet.”

Adele’s chart-topping song “Hello” deals with the pain of reconnecting with someone who ghosted you in the past.

“Hello” is a chart-topping ballad by British singer-songwriter Adele, released in 2015 as the lead single from her third studio album, “25.”

The song about ghosting was co-written with producer Greg Kurstin and is known for its powerful vocals and emotional lyrics.

It was a massive commercial success, topping charts in multiple countries and breaking records for the most viewed music video in 24 hours on YouTube.

5.Blow Me One Last Kiss by P!nk

White knuckles and sweaty palms from hanging on too tight.” P!nk’s energetic anthem “Blow Me One Last Kiss” deals with the anger and frustration of being ghosted, urging the ghoster to “blow me one last kiss.”

Blow Me One Last Kiss is a high-energy pop rock song by American singer-songwriter P!nk.

The song was released as the lead single from her sixth studio album, “The Truth About Love,” in 2012.

It was written by P!nk herself, along with Butch Walker and Greg Kurstin, and features an upbeat tempo, catchy hooks, and empowering lyrics that address the frustration and anger of being rejected and abandoned by a former lover.

6.Liability by Lorde

Baby really hurt me, crying in the taxi.” Lorde’s haunting ballad “Liability” deals with the feelings of isolation and self-doubt that come with being ghosted.

“Liability” is a haunting ballad by New Zealand singer-songwriter Lorde, featured on her second studio album, “Melodrama.”

The song  about ghosting explores the feelings of isolation and self-doubt that come with being left behind after a relationship ends.

Lorde has stated that the song was inspired by her own experiences of feeling like a burden to others and struggling with self-acceptance.

7.Call Me!!! by Olivia O’Brien

”I know that you see my calls, I know that you hear my texts.” Olivia O’Brien’s upbeat pop song “Call Me!!!” deals with the frustration of being ignored after being ghosted, urging the ghoster to pick up the phone.

“Call Me!!!” is an upbeat pop song by American singer-songwriter Olivia O’Brien.

The track was released in 2019 as part of her debut album “Was It Even Real?”.

The song about ghosting deals with the frustration and confusion of being ignored after being ghosted by someone you care about

8.Where Were You In The Morning? by Shawn Mendes

“You got me deep in my feelings.” Shawn Mendes’ smooth R&B track “Where Were You In The Morning?” deals with the confusion and hurt caused by waking up alone after being ghosted.

“Where Were You In The Morning?” is a song about ghosting by Canadian singer-songwriter Shawn Mendes.

The track was released as a single from his self-titled third studio album in 2018.

The song is a mix of pop, R&B, and soulful ballad, with Mendes’ signature falsetto and catchy beats.

It deals with the themes of loneliness, heartbreak, and the feeling of waking up alone after being ghosted by someone you thought was special

9.The Night We Met by Lord Huron

“I am not the one you want.” Lord Huron’s wistful folk song “The Night We Met” deals with the lingering feelings of loss and regret that come with being ghosted.

The Night We Met is a hauntingly beautiful folk song by the American indie band Lord Huron.

It was released in 2015 as part of the band’s second album, “Strange Trails.”

The song gained widespread popularity after it was featured in the Netflix series “13 Reasons Why” in 2017.

The lyrics describe a nostalgic yearning for a lost love and the lingering memories of a past relationship

10.Ghost Town By Kanye West

This song is from Kanye West’s eighth studio album, “Ye,” released on June 1, 2018.

The song about ghosting been described as a genre-bending track, blending elements of rock, rap, and gospel music.

The lyrics deal with themes of mental health, addiction, and loneliness.

The song features Kid Cudi and 070 Shake, who both provide powerful vocals and add depth to the emotional weight of the track.

Ghost Town has been praised for its unique sound and the vulnerability expressed in its lyrics.
