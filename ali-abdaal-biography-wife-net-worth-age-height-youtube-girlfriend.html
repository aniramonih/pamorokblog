Early Life

Ali Abdaal was born in Pakistan on May 11, 1994, and spent some time in Lesotho before returning to his home country.

In 2003, he moved to the United Kingdom with his mother and brother, where their single mother raised them. Ali’s mother is a doctor, and his younger brother, Taimur Abdaal, founded ‘Causal,’ a business planning platform.

Their journey from Pakistan to Lesotho and back, finally settling in the UK, showcases their family’s resilience and determination to create a better life for themselves.

Education

Ali’s educational journey began at Westcliff High School for Boys in Southend-on-Sea, England, where he completed his schooling in 2012.

He then pursued a Bachelor of Arts (B.A.) in preclinical medicine and psychology at the University of Cambridge, transitioning from his initial interest in computer science to a passion for medicine.

Upon graduating in 2015, Ali continued his studies at the same university to earn his MBBS degree, which he completed in 2018.

His academic pursuits reflect his dedication to medicine and his commitment to furthering his knowledge and skills in this noble profession.

Career

Ali’s YouTube channel saw exponential growth after his video ‘How to study for exams – Evidence-based revision tips’ went viral, marking a turning point for his content creation.

On September 7 2021, he launched his podcast YouTube channel ‘Deep Dive with Ali Abdaal’, where he interviewed popular YouTubers such as Marques Brownlee and Max Fosh.

Alongside his main channel, Ali also manages other channels like ‘Ali Abdaal – Appendix’ and ‘Not Overthinking,’ all while juggling a full-time job as a doctor.
