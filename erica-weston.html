Erica Weston Biography

Erica Weston is an American sports presenter and journalist who was born and brought up in Lone Tree, Colorado, United States of America. Currently, she works at Bally Sports West(formerly Fox Sports Midwest) in Los Angeles, California, as a host and reporter. She also worked for CBS Studios as a sports anchor. Previously, Weston worked as a host and reporter at Bally Sports Midwest | Bally Sports Kansas City | Bally Sports Indiana in St. Louis, Missouri for three years.

Erica worked at KGUN TV in Tucson, Arizona, as a sports anchor and reporter, and at Los Angeles Dodgers as a reporter and host. She served as a studio technician and playback operator for Time Warner Cable Sports Net, and as a sideline reporter for Los Angeles D-Fenders.

Erica is a former Denver Broncos Cheerleader and L.A. Lakers, dancer. Furthermore, she is a self-proclaimed” beauty geek, she is a contributor to the beauty blog at ” Victoria’s Vanity”.
