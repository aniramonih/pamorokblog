Early life and Education

Lera Auerbach was born on October 21, 1973, in Chelyabinsk, Russia, into a Jewish family deeply rooted in musical tradition. In 1991, Auerbach moved to the United States, where she furthered her education at the prestigious Juilliard School in New York.

She also expanded her academic horizons by studying comparative literature at Columbia University, enriching her artistic outlook and adding depth to her musical compositions.
