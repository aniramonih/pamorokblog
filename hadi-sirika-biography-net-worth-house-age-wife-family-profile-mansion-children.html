Biography

Hadi Abubakar Sirika (born March 2, 1964), popularly identified as Hadi Sirika, is a Nigerian politician and aviator with a remarkable career. 

Serving as the Minister of Aviation for the Federal Republic of Nigeria from 2015 to 2023, Hadi Sirika is a committed member of the All Progressives Congress (APC) party. He has played a pivotal role in influencing and advancing the aviation sector in Nigeria, making significant contributions to the nation’s overall development.

Career

Hadi Sirika entered the political arena in 2003 and successfully won a position in the Federal House of Representatives. Throughout his tenure, he showed a remarkable grasp of aviation matters and actively worked towards advancing the industry. His dedication and proficiency were acknowledged, resulting in his subsequent election as a Senator in 2011.

In 2015, former President Muhammadu Buhari made a significant decision by appointing Hadi Sirika as the Minister of State for Aviation. This appointment marked a crucial turning point in Sirika‘s career, as he was entrusted with overseeing and managing Nigeria’s aviation sector. With this new role, Sirika was tasked with ensuring the smooth functioning and development of the nation’s air transportation system.

Under the leadership of Sirika, the aviation industry in Nigeria has experienced a significant and impressive transformation. This can be attributed to his implementation of an all-encompassing strategy that emphasizes the development of infrastructure, enhancement of safety measures, and reformulation of policies. Hadi Sirika has propelled the Nigerian aviation sector towards greater growth and progress through these concerted efforts.

In his interview with journalists Soni Daniel and Ruth Choji, Sirika, a renowned pilot and a close associate of former President Muhammadu Buhari, expressed his concern that despite leading the country for the past 13 years, his party has failed to come up with new ideas to advance the nation. 

Representing the Katsina North senatorial district of Katsina State, Hadi Sirika emphasized the need for credible and responsive leadership in Nigeria. He also discussed his plans to revive and rescue the struggling Nigerian aviation industry.
