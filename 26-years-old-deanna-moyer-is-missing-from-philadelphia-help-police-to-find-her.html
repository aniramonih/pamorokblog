26-years-old Deanna Moyer is missing from Philadelphia: Help police to find her

 Posted on August 12, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  256 words  |  Billy Koelling

Philadelphia, PA – The Philadelphia Police Department is reaching out to the public for crucial assistance in locating Deanna Moyer, a 26-year-old white woman reported missing after last being seen on Sunday evening, December 17, 2023, at her residence in the 1800 block of Belfield Ave.

Missing Person Details:
Name: Deanna Moyer
Age: 26
Date of Birth: November 24, 1997
Race: White
Sex: Female
Height: 5’06”
Weight: 130 pounds
Hair color: Brown
Eye color: Hazel
Last Seen: 1800 block of Belfield Ave, Germantown area, on Sunday evening, December 17, 2023, at approximately 5:00 pm

Deanna was reportedly seen driving a black Acura TLX with a Pennsylvania license plate MCF4182. Her attempt to seek assistance due to a car breakdown resulted in her phone being discovered at a nearby bodega.

Law enforcement is actively engaged in the search, but any information, sightings, or security camera footage from residents in the vicinity would be immensely valuable. Deanna may also be in the Francisville area.

For any information, please contact the Central Detective Division at 215-686-3093 or 215-686-3094. Detectives Winward (#8070) and Lyons (#840) are leading the investigation, and you can also reach out via email at Gerald.Winward@Phila.gov or Robert.Lyons@Phila.gov. Please reference the case number DC# 23-22-073137 and CDD# 23-12532.The Philadelphia Police Department urges the community to come together and share any information that might aid in locating Deanna Moyer. Your assistance is vital in helping bring her back to her loved ones.

As the search intensifies, let’s collectively contribute to finding Deanna and ensuring her safe return.
