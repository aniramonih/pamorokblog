Scott Padgett CBS 11

Scott Padgett joined CBS 11 in September 2013. Scott’s interest in weather started at a young age. He would sit with his father on the front porch watching the storms roll into his neighborhood. Thanks to his dad, his fear transformed into an interest, leading him to study meteorology at Western Illinois University, where he graduated with a B.S in meteorology and a double minor in broadcasting and math.
