Birth Name: Stacy Ann-Marie Keibler

Place of Birth: Rosedale, Maryland, U.S.

Date of Birth: October 14, 1979

Ethnicity: German, other

Stacy Keibler is an American cheerleader, professional wrestler and valet, actress, dancer, and model. She has worked with WCW and WWE. She has also been known as Miss Hancock, Skye, and Super Stacy.

Stacy is the daughter of Patricia and Gary Keibler. In 1990, she won Miss Maryland Pre-Teen, and then National Miss Pre-Teen Crown. She is married to Future Ads CEO, Jared Pobre, with whom she has three children.
