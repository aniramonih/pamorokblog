Who is Al Pacino’s sister Roberta?

Roberta Pacino: Bio Summary

Who is Al Pacino’s sister Roberta?

Roberta Pacino is one of the younger siblings of the iconic actor, Al Pacino. Per Famechain, she was born in 1953 and she is 70 years old as of 2023. the daughter of  Sal Pacino and his second wife Corrine Pacino. Roberta is the half-sister of the famous actor. She had two other biological siblings and two half-siblings including Al. Her biological siblings are Josette Pacino and Paula Pacino. Al and Desiree Pacino are her two half-siblings.

Roberta Pacino is a writer and producer

Al has graced the screens of fans playing different roles but he received the Academy Award for Best Actor for his role in Scent of a Woman (1992). His Oscar-nominated roles also include The Godfather (1972), Serpico (1973), The Godfather Part II (1974), Dog Day Afternoon (1975), and …And Justice for All (1979), Dick Tracy (1990), Glengarry Glen Ross (1992), and The Irishman (2019).

While his big brother is grabbing awards and nominations left and right, Roberta was also penning down her name in her own way. According to her IMDB profile, she is a writer and producer known for Behind the Rain: The Story of a Museum (2003) and The Shiver Shack (2000). She also named her company, “Quarter to Three Films”, after the first line in the Frank Sinatra song “One For My Baby”, one of her father’s favorites.

Roberta is a married woman

Al Pacino is still a bachelor in his old age but is a father of 3 lovely children. He has been in relationships with several lovely women, but he has never married any of them. Roberta Pacino on the other hand is a married woman. She is married to Mark Richman. The couple have worked together on many projects.

“Behind The Rain: The Story of a Museum,” the 52-minute film produced by the husband-and-wife duo of Mark Richman and Roberta Pacino, tells the story of the museum and “how we had probably the worst storage conditions of any museum and went to the best conditions of any museum anywhere in the country,” said Michael Mares. Richman and Roberta Pacino were students at the University of Oklahoma when Richman made a documentary about outhouses, “The Shiver Shack,” for a class assignment- Oklahoman reported.

Roberta Pacino told the outlet, she and her husband Richman have tried to avoid using their obvious connections in the film industry while trying to build an independent film company called Quarter to Three Films Inc. It seems entertainment runs in the Pacino family as their father Sal also had some film credits to his name.

The half-siblings also appear to be very close as they have been seen taking pictures at events together. Roberta Pacino even went to support her brother at the 35th AFI Life Achievement Award tribute to Al Pacino held at the Kodak Theatre on June 7, 2007, in Hollywood, California.

Unfortunately Al and Roberta’s father passed away in 2005. He was described by many as a colorful and bright man who lived life to the fullest. Roberta’s father passed away on January 12, 2005, at the age of 82 in Covina, California, USA. He was laid to rest at Forest Lawn Memorial ParkCovina, Los Angeles County, California, USA.
