Nicole da Silva Movies and TV Shows

Da Silva’s first on-screen appearance was in the medical drama All Saints on the Seven Network in 2005, where she played Sasha Fernandez for 11 episodes. That role helped her land future roles, and in 2007, da Silva starred as Erica ‘EC’ Eulestra in the Fox8 series Dangerous, for which she received a Logie Award nomination for her performance in the story of forbidden love set in Sydney’s culturally diverse backgrounds.

Da Silva rose to prominence as Stella Dagostino in the four-season Network Ten police drama Rush, in which she played Stella Dagostino in the story based on the real-life Victoria Police Critical Incident Response Team, which is similar to the SWAT team in the United States, with the plot exploring both the team’s personal and professional lives. Meanwhile, Nicole played Lisa Testro in Carla Cametti PD (2009) for six episodes, but the show was canceled after only one season.
