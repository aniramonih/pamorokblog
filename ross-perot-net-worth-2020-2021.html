Early Life

Henry Ross Perot was born in 1930 on 27 June to Lula May Perot and Gabriel Ross Perot. He was born in Texarkana, Texas. His father was a commodity broker who specialized in cotton contracts.

Perot attended a private school by the name Patty Hill. He later joined Texas High School, situated in Texarkana in 1947. Between 1947 and 1949, Ross studied at Texarkana junior college. After that, he joined the US Naval academy.
