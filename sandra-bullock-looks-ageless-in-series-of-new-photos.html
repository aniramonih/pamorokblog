Sandra Bullock Looks Like She Hasnt Aged A Day During Recent Appearances See The Pics!

 Posted on August 8, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  328 words  |  Kelle Repass

Looking good, Sandra Bullock! The actress always looks fabulous, but during her latest few public appearances, the movie star has looked better than ever. Closer Weekly once chatted with experts (who have never directly worked with Sandra) about her potential plastic surgery procedures, and they revealed her stunning look could be due to Botox.

“It looks like Sandra is a regular user of Botox as far back as 2006. Back then, she was probably getting too much and whoever injected her [face] arched her eyebrows too much. This made her eyes look angry,” Dr. Adam H. Hamawy, MD, FACS suggested to Closer.

Dr. Vartan Mardirossian — a leading board certified facial plastic surgeon and world-renowned facial feminization surgeon in Jupiter, FL — also suggested, “Her forehead is smooth and her eyebrows are nicely arched, suggesting that she might have had Botox… She has lost some volume of the lower cheeks… normally that would be accompanied with loss of volume if the cheekbones (or high cheeks) but we do not quite see that.”

In a recent interview, the 54-year-old jokingly “revealed” her secret to looking young. “I’m sleeping in a container that is devoid of outside carcinogens, that helps lift what you don’t have and — come on, please!” Sandra teased to ET. “The only time you see me is when I do press junkets. Do you know how many hours of hair and makeup this took? Two and a half hours!”

After kidding around, she then admitted her “secret” to looking flawless is putting in a lot of time and effort. “I’m gonna work this look all day today and then I’m gonna go home and take a shower, and it’s gonna happen again at 8 o’clock in the morning,” Sandra explained. “I’m never out in the world where I get photographed unless it’s in things like that, so I just look my best.” We think you look fabulous, girl!
