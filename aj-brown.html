Who is AJ Brown?

AJ Brown , sometimes known as Arthur Juan Brown, plays wide receiver for the National Football League’s Tennessee Titans.

During his time there, Brown has improved the Titans’ offense, giving them explosiveness in the air in addition to their strength on the ground. Brown, who is still quite new to the game, has established himself as one of the bright young stars at wide receiver.

Football career of AJ Brown

High School (Starkville High School)

At Starkville High School in Starkville, Mississippi, A.J. Brown played high school football. He was skilled in both sports and also played baseball there. Brown participated in the baseball and football Under Armour All-American competitions.

His team’s state title victory was aided by his 13 touchdown receptions as a senior. After being selected by the San Diego Padres in the 2016 MLB Draft, he decided to forgo playing college baseball in favor of collegiate football at Ole Miss.

AJ Brown’s Career in College (Ole Miss Rebels)

Brown spent three seasons as a member of the Ole Miss football team and will go down as one of the school’s all-time greatest receivers.

In a victory over South Alabama as a sophomore, he set the Ole Miss record most receiving yards in a game with 233 yards. He averaged more than 1,250 yards and five touchdowns per season in his final two seasons.

Brown decided to forego his senior year and join the NFL Draft following a junior season in which he accumulated 1,320 yards.

The 51st overall pick in the 2019 NFL Draft went to A.J. Brown. The Tennessee Titans selected him in the second round of the draft.

Brown hauled in 52 passes for 1,051 yards and eight touchdowns during his debut season. In just 10 games of his second season in the league, he matched that number of touchdowns, displaying a trajectory of development that has many believing Brown will remain an outstanding receiver in the NFL for years to come.
