What is a good starting weapon for a Paladin?

A Moon-Touched Sword is a common item that makes a perfect starting weapon for a Paladin. It’s a two-handed greatsword that does 2d6 slashing damage.

What is the best starting equipment for a Paladin DND?

Armor: Paladins start with chain mail and move on to full-plate, and those that use one-handed weapons should also equip a shield. Weapons: Part of the Paladin build is selecting the character’s Weapon Mastery, so the choice depends entirely on what role the character takes in the party.

What weapon should a Paladin use 5e?

So, once again, the weapon for your 5e Paladin hack depends on what flavor of Paladin you prefer. If you are using a shield, go longsword, warhammer, lance, or spear. Longsword and warhammer have the added bonus of being versatile (you can use them as two-handed weapons if you want to be super cool).

What weapons can Paladins not use?

Paladin cannot equip Ranged Weapons (Bows, Crossbows, Guns, Wands), Polearms, Thrown Weapons, 1H, and 2H Swords. Besides, Paladin cannot dual wield melee weapons.

What do paladins start with?

You start with the following equipment, in addition to the equipment granted by your background:
– (a) a martial weapon and a shield or (b) two martial weapons.
– (a) five javelins or (b) any simple melee weapon.
– (a) a priest’s pack or (b) an explorer’s pack.
– Chain mail and a holy symbol.

Can a paladin have no God?

Paladins need not serve a God, but many do. Point of interest: even if the Paladin serves a god, that god does not grant the Paladin their spells.

What is the best Paladin starter weapon 5E?

That means there are even more choices for the best Paladin weapons in D&D 5e. Here are some options:
1. Defender: A powerful weapon that grants a bonus to AC and saving throws.
2. Sun Blade: A radiant energy weapon that deals extra damage against undead.
3. Oathsunder: A magical sword that can channel the Paladin’s divine smite.
4. Mace Of Disruption: A mace that deals extra damage against undead and can destroy them with a critical hit.
5. The Vorpal Sword: A legendary sword that can instantly kill a creature on a critical hit.
6. Holy Avenger: A magical longsword that grants extra abilities to the Paladin.
7. Conquering Maul: A powerful warhammer that deals extra damage against creatures with resistance or immunity to bludgeoning damage.
8. Sword Of Zariel: A devilish sword that deals additional fire damage and can teleport the Paladin.
