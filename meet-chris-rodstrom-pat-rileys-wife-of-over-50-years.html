Pat Riley’s Wife, Chris Rodstrom, is a Native of San Diego, California

Chris Rodstrom and Pat Riley Began Dating in 1967 and Wed in 1970

Chris Rodstrom first met her future husband, Pat Riley, in San Diego in 1967. At that time, he was playing for the professional basketball team in the city while she was still studying. They fell in love with each other and started dating, and their relationship blossomed from the get-go.

Rodstrom introduced her boyfriend to her family members and they accepted him. They also became passionate basketball fans and joined her in supporting him. Rodstrom and Riley dated for a few years before getting engaged and tying the knot.
