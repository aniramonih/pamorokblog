Before the glitz and glamor, Oteri worked at A&M Records, a far cry from the comedy stages she was destined to conquer. It was her move to Los Angeles and subsequent involvement with The Groundlings that set the stage for her stardom.

Some of her most famous characters include the hyperactive cheerleader Spartan Spirit, the overly enthusiastic office worker Rita DelVecchio, and the quirky talk show host Arianna, the Spartan Cheerleader.
