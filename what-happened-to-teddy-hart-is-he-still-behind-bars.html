What Happened To Teddy Hart? Is He Still Behind Bars?

 Posted on August 25, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  459 words  |  Tandra Barner

Teddy Hart is a Canadian-American professional wrestler. His birth name is Edward Ellsworth Annis, and he was born on February 2nd, 1980. 

Teddy Hart was arrested for assaulting his girlfriend back in 2020.

This is not the first time the wrestler has been imprisoned. 

The wrestler has made a name for himself in the wrestling world, but that is not the only skill that has made him famous. 

Teddy has faced charges of injuring a child/elderly/disabled person, evading arrest with a vehicle, and possession of a controlled substance. 

The athlete has been surrounded by many legal cases and arrested multiple times.

Teddy was detained in Virginia in February 2020 and accused of possessing a schedule III controlled substance with the intent to resell or distribute it.

The wrestler was detained once more in Virginia on March 4, 2020, for failing to adhere to the terms of his home arrest.

After reportedly assaulting his girlfriend, female independent wrestler Maria Manic, at the home of a fellow independent wrestler, Ace Montana, Hart was detained for the third time in Virginia on March 26, 2020.

When Hart was arrested for the third time, he was accused of strangling and causing bodily damage to his then-girlfriend by fellow wrestler Ace Montana. 

Despite being imprisoned multiple times, Teddy has somehow always managed to get bail until the next time he gets arrested. It is a vicious cycle that does not seem to end anytime soon. 

Samantha Fiddler, Teddy’s ex-girlfriend, disappeared in Florida on November 19, 2016, while undergoing professional wrestling training.

Despite not reaching a conclusion even after conducting a comprehensive investigation, the general opinion was that Teddy had something to do with her disappearance. 

Nothing can come between Teddy and his passion for wrestling, as the professional wrestler is still wrestling on the independent circuit despite repeatedly being in and out of jail. 

Teddy Harts age, biography and net worth

Teddy Hart turned 43 years old this year and currently wrestles on the independent circuit. 

He wrestled on a National scale for Major League Wrestling (MLW) as the leader of The Hart Foundation. 

He operates a wrestling school in Edmonton. He is the son of Georgia Hart of the Hart wrestling family and wrestler B. J. Annis. 

Wrestling runs in Hart’s blood and soul, as his uncles, Bret Hart and Owen Hart, were also professional wrestlers. Hart achieved early fame when he became the youngest wrestler to be signed to the World Wrestling Federation (now WWE).

According to some sources, Hart’s estimated net worth is $5 million. Teddy earns from his wrestling career as well as from endorsements. 

Teddy seems to firmly believe in the phrase ‘any press is good press’ since the wrestler manages to bring himself into the news occasionally.
