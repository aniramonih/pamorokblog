Who are Katt Williams' ex-wives and does he have any kids?

 Posted on August 25, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  592 words  |  Tandra Barner

KATT WILLIAMS made headlines in January 2024 with his appearance on Shannon Sharpe's podcast, leaving fans with questions about his personal life.

In the interview - which racked up over 9 million YouTube views in less than 24 hours - comedian and actor Williams spoke about many topics, including his beef with fellow comedian Kevin Hart.

Who are Katt Williams' ex-wives?

Quadirah Locus

Quadirah Locus was the first wife of comedian Kat Williams.

The pair welcomed one child together during their relationship.

Locus had a number of children prior to her marriage to Williams and it has been reported that Williams adopted these children, the half-brothers and sisters of his son.

Williams and Locus are now divorced.

Eboni Gray

Williams was reportedly in a "legal union" with a woman named Eboni Gray following his divorce from his first wife.

In 2016, TMZ reported that Gray had asked the courts to annul her alleged domestic partnership with the comedian.

She claimed that "they were together, in the eyes of the law, from 2010 to 2011", according to the publication.

Williams denied Gray's claims, saying there was no romantic involvement between the pair.

Williams has had several other high-profile relationships, including romances with Jhonni Blaze, Arica Kane, Hazel-E and Maryse Selit.

Does Katt Williams have any children?

Williams has eight children.

He is father to his biological son Micah, who he shares with ex-wife Quadirah Locus, and also has seven adopted kids.

Williams once told Howard Stern that the reason he has adopted so many children is because of a promise he once made himself.

Before making it in big in comedy, he vowed to help others as much as he could, which is now why he has so many kids in his life.

What is Katt Williams' net worth?

According to Celebrity Net Worth, Williams has a net worth of around $2million.

Williams has, however, claimed that his fortune is much larger.

In his January 2024 interview with Shannon Sharpe, Williams claimed that he made almost $10million just from his 2022 Netflix comedy special World War III.

He also said that he can earn over $100,000 for every comedy show he performs, suggesting he has a lot more than $2million in the bank.

Williams got his start in comedy after moving to Los Angeles in 1999.

The star, who hitchhiked to Florida after becoming emancipated from his parents at the age of 13, performed at comedy clubs like The Improv and Ice House in his early career.

He then then landed his first comedy special, Let a Playa Play, in 2006 and this was shortly followed up by another special on HBO, The Pimp Chronicles Pt. 1.

What movies has Katt Williams starred in?

Williams' first movie role was in 2002's Friday After Next, which he starred in with Ice Cube and Mike Epps.

His other credits include Epic Movie, Norbit, Scary Movie 5 and School Dance.

He has also appeared in many TV shows throughout his career, including My Wife and Kids, Atlanta, Girlfriends and Black-ish.

In 2018 he won a Primetime Emmy Award for Outstanding Guest Actor in a Comedy Series for his role in Donald Glover's Atlanta.

Williams hit the headlines in January 2024 thanks to claims he made on Sharpe's podcast Club Shay Shay.
