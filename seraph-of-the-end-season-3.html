Seraph Of The End Season 3: Is It Officially Renewed or Not?

In Japan, the manga series ‘Seraph Of the End,’ also known as ‘Owari no Serafu,’ is written and drawn by Takaya Kagami and Daisuke Furuya. Yamato Yamamoto represents a popular anime series. “Seraph of the End” was published for the first time on September 3, 2012.

The first season debuted on April 4, 2015. Fans of anime fell in love with it and became fascinated with it. The second season began later that year, and since then, fans have eagerly anticipated the third season.

Seraph Of The End Season 3 Release Date

There is currently no information on the release date or production status of Seraph Of The End season 3. The extended wait is partly because the first two seasons of Seraph Of The End covered practically all of Takayo Kagami’s manga volumes.

Season 3 of Seraph Of The End was canceled because Wit Studio (Attack on Titan) could not locate any more manga to adapt. Kagami has provided almost 40 additional chapters to the Seraph Of The End manga series, ensuring that Wit Studios has enough material for a third season.

Seraph Of The End Season 3 Plot

The anime presents a fictitious universe and focuses on a young man named Yuuichiro Amane. Amane’s history is terrible since he lost both of his parents early. While striving to acclimate and live a better life at the Hyakuyu Orphanage, the human race is struck by a catastrophic event.

Suddenly, a fatal disease spreads across the planet, killing everyone older than 13. At the same time, children mourn the loss of their loved ones, and vampires that had laid dormant under the surface rise to dominate the globe by enslaving humanity and becoming its overlords.

Fortunately, Yuuichirou can escape the massacre and return to the surface. When he reaches the surface, he is spotted by the Moon Demon Company of the Imperial Demon Army, a ferocious human group whose main aim is to eradicate vampires forever, liberating humans from enslavement.

The narrative changes when Yuuichirou reunites with Mikaela, whom he believes to be dead. Their reunion was not joyous; Mikaela had also transformed into a vampire, to his astonishment.

Yuuichirou has even more reasons to loathe vampires, and he vows to become stronger and destroy the vampire world that stole his human buddy and caused misery to the world. He immediately joins the Imperial Demon Army and begins training to kill vampires.
