He developed his guitar talents as a result of his early interest in music and started writing songs that drew inspiration from local Mexican music. With the publication of his first two studio albums, “Ah y Qué?” in 2020 and “Efectos Secundarios” in 2021, Pluma made a breakthrough.

His distinct style and engaging lyrical talent were introduced to listeners with these albums, which had a little amount of success. But the song “El Belicón” he made in 2022 with Raul Vega is what really launched him to fame. The RIAA certified “El Belicón” as 8 Platinum Latin after it sold an amazing 480,000 units and immediately became a hit.

Peso Pluma started music in his teens and has a growing career

Peso Pluma, who has been in the entertainment industry since 2020, is a rising musician from Mexico.

Best-known for singing traditional Mexican ballads, he also specialises in a subgenre of contemporary takes on the ballad, known as narcocorridos. Most of his lyrics recount the exploits of drug traffickers.

He was born Hassan Emilio Kabande Laija on June 15, 1999, in Zapopan, Mexico.

He is also known by the monikers La Doble P, PP, and Doble P.

While the names of his parents aren’t known, they are both Mexicans; his father has Lebanese ancestry and was born in Chiapas.

Other family members and relatives are residents of Badiraguato, Sinaloa.

The 24-year-old Mexican musician and singer started his career when he was a teenager. He was inspired by his country’s rich musical tradition and started developing his musical talents as a result. He picked up the guitar in his adolescence, began improving his technique, and experimented with various melodies and lyrics.

Just like many A-list artist, Pluma was mocked by his peers when he began writing songs and and singing to them.

Describing his initial approach to songwriting, he explained how writing became his therapy: “That’s where I’d write how I was feeling, then I realized some stuff would rhyme. I kept practicing and became better with time.”

Currently signed to Record labels like El Cartel De Los Angeles and Label Warner Music Latina, Pluma does genres like Regional Mexican; Latin music; corrido tumbado; banda music; Latin trap; reggaeton; country pop.

He has several albums and singles in his credit.

On February 2022, he gained momentum on TikTok for his hit single “El Belicon”, a corrido anthem on the local narcoculture. He worked on this masterpiece with Raul Vega and the music video, which the artists portrayed the roles of members of a drug cartels, garnered over 10 million views on YouTube.

In April, “El Belicón” debuted at number 50 on Billboard Hot Latin Songs, peaking at number 46 two weeks after and becoming Pluma’s first entry on the charts.

Peso Pluma recently earned a much more international audience thanks to his performance at the 2023 MTV VMAs. He received several nominations, and performed his song “Lady Gaga.” Just days later, though, the future of his entire tour seems to be up in the air.
