Christi Zook Lukasiak Wiki Biography

Christi Zook Lukasiak was born on the 3rd March 1977, in Churchill, Pennsylvania USA, and is a reality TV star, best known to the world for her appearance in the reality dance competition show “Dance Moms” (2011-2017).

Christi has a younger sister, Jodi, and the two were raised by their maternal grandparents in Groton, Connecticut. Christi’s relationship with her mother became estranged over the years, mostly because her mother didn’t have time for her, and sent her to live with grandparents in a small house near Groton. There, she fell in love with dance as she would often dance with her younger sister. Unfortunately, her childhood was marred by poverty and other struggles, so she wasn’t capable of pursuing a career as a dancer.

However, luck smiled upon her, when she enrolled her daughter Chloe at Abby Lee Dance Company. The two later became a part of the reality dance competition show “Dance Moms” (2011-2017).

Christi also enrolled her second daughter Clara, and she also joined them in the cast of the highly acclaimed show. Unfortunately, the trio left the show in its fourth season, due to an argument between Christi and Abby, after Abby bullied Chloe. She has then taken Chloe to another dance studio – The Studio 19 Dance Complex – where her young daughter has continued pursuing both her and Christi’s dreams.

Regarding her personal life, Christi has been married to Marc Lukasiak, who is a director of marketing and communications at the Children’s Hospital of Pittsburgh. The couple has two daughters together, Chloe, and Clara. They now divide their time between residences in Pennsylvania and Los Angeles.
