Career

Hanks’s career kicked off in 1979 when he appeared in the film He Knows You’re Alone (1980) and Mazes and Monsters. He then landed a leading role in ABC television show Bosom Buddies, Splash 1984, and Bachelors Party 1984.

Hanks’s stature in the box office industry rose when he appeared in Dragnet, a comedy film, Big 1988, Punchline, and Turner & Hooch. His legacy of success as an actor continued with him appearing in films such as A League of Their Own 1992, Philadelphia 1993, Apollo 13 1995, Saving Private Ryan 1998, The Green Mile 1999, among others.

Hanks international recognition as an actor, screenwriter, and producer was achieved when he produced My Big Fat Greek Wedding 2002 and acted on Catch Me If You Can 2002, followed by The Ladykillers 2004. His latest works in filmography are films such as Small Fry 2011, Cloud Atlas 2012, Captain Phillips 2013, Bridge of Spies 2015, My Big Fat Greek Wedding 2 2016, The Circle 2017, among many others.

Other than films, Hanks is a supporter of space programs by NASA and has even released an iOS app Hanx Writer.
