Who is Kay Flock?

Kay Flock, whose real name is Kevin Perez, is a young American rapper from the Bronx. He became famous in 2020 with songs like “Opp Spotter” and “Shot Down.”

He’s also known for his song Not in the Mood, which made it to number 61 on the Billboard Hot 100 chart. Kay Flock released his first mixtape, The D.O.A. Tape, in 2021.

In the music world, Kay Flock is known for his great singing and rapping skills. His song Brotherly Love got a lot of attention too. He sometimes goes by just “Kay” in the music industry. He’s part of a rap group called Thraxxx, and he also makes music on his own.

Overall, Kay Flock is a young rapper from the Bronx who became famous with his music, especially Not in the Mood. But he’s also facing some legal trouble, and people are hoping for the best for him.
