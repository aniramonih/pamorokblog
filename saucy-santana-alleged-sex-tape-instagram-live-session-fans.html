Saucy Santana denies sex tape claims, trolls say '2021 really going out with a bang'

 Posted on August 15, 2024  |  4 minutes  |  682 words  |  Billy Koelling

Hours after Hollywood iconic star Betty White died at the age of 99, the Internet quickly changed its direction to Saucy Santana’s alleged sex tape that apparently hit the web on Friday, December 31. A video leaked online that reportedly showed the ‘Material Girl’ hitmaker engaging in sexual relations with another man, as reported by Hot New HipHop. The clip apparently made it to Twitter and a few other websites as social media users could not help but talk about it.
 
Looking at the hype, Saucy Santana denied all the claims with the help of an Instagram Live session. The 28-year-old rapper claimed that he is not the one shown in the video. He defended his case by saying that if it really was him, he would have tried to make money off the footage. Many on Twitter could not find Saucy Santana’s alleged sex tape. Santana had earlier made news for reigniting beef with Rolling Ray after poking fun at him as they got engaged in a hilarious banter.

Saucy Santana denies sex tape claims

The official Instagram page of The Shade Room shared multiple clips of Saucy Santana where he cleared the air on one of the hottest topics on the last day of the year. In the video, Santana says, “So on my right side, right here, real big and ghetto. That's a dollar sign, and it's a cheetah print – that's how old it was. I was a young ghetto girl at 18. I have a tattoo on my right side, right here. Any (N-word) that's ever been in bed with me knows that, and of my friends, that's ever seen me in panties or naked knows that."
 
The Florida native continued, “So, when you looking at the video, and you see the boy pull out from the back, and you see him shift his camera to the right, don't say, 'oh, something, the edits, the angle...'. If the angle was edited, the video would be flipped, it would look weird.” He further denied the claims by adding, “You see him hitting the boy from the back, and you see him turn the camera to the right side. This is my left. This is my right. We gon' turn to my right.” Continuing clearing his name in the matter, the rapper stated, “I know y'all probably would like to have thought that it was Santana, and it probably would've been a thing if it was Santana, but it's not Santana. So I can't help y'all with that."

‘Santana just couldn’t let Betty White have her moment’, says Twitter

The next one shared, “How Twitter went from mourning Betty White to Saucy Santana leaked video.” Meanwhile, one said, “Lmao twitter went from mourning Betty White to Saucy Santana back shot video. What a wild place to be.” One individual asserted, “Ok where is Santana video Rolling on the floor laughing I want to see, period”, as another concluded, “Santana said that was not him y'all. He need to find his twin cuz I wanna see.”
