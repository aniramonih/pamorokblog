Annamarie Tendler is an American makeup artist, hairstylist and writer. However, she is better known as the ex-wife of American Primetime Emmy Award-winning stand-up comedian and actor, John Mulaney. He is popular for his work as a writer on the late-night live TV sketch comedy show, Saturday Night Live.

Tendler has worked for several magazines such as Glamour, HelloGiggles, MTV Styles, and DailyCandy. Also, she has her own handcrafts Victorian-style lampshades business named Silk Parlor.

When Annamarie and John got Hitched?

The 35 years old Tendler met her future husband John on their vacation to Martha's Vineyard in Massachusetts in the late 2000s. On February 16, 2010, she announced that he is the man she's going to marry according to Insider.

In January 2012, Mulaney also mentioned that his girlfriend is a makeup artist who cuts his hair for free. Finally, the lovebirds exchanged the vows on  July 5, 2014, at the Onteora Mountain House  Boiceville, New York. 

Their woodland-deco theme wedding was performed by comedian Dan Levy. "He married a beautiful and perfect woman. He wishes he could live it over and over again", he wrote on his Instagram sharing a wedding picture to mark the occasion.

John Filed a Divorce From Annamarie Following His Rehab Stint

Yes, the makeup artist, Tendler and her husband Mulaney's relationship have to come to it end. According to Page Six, the comedian has asked for a divorce from his wife three months ago.

It is reported that Mulaney filed a divorce petition on May 10, 2021, after six years of marriage. John would like to continues to focus on his recovery and getting back to work and has not any further comment, the source added.

 It has come after he struggled with alcohol and drug abuse into rehab for 60 days. Anna Marie Tendler also told Page Six that she's heartbroken that he has decided to end their marriage. But still, she wishes him support and success as he continues his recovery.
