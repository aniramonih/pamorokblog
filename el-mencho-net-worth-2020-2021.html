Early Life & Biography

El Mencho was born on July 17, 1966, in the rural community of Naranjo de Chila, which is in Aguililla, Michoacán, Mexico. He has some alternative aliases, which are “Rubén Acerguera Cervantes,” “Lorenzo Mendoza,” “Nemecio,” and “Nemesio Oseguera Ramos.” It was reported that his birth-name was Ruben, and he changed his name to Nemesio in the memory of his grandfather.

El Mencho grew up in a relatively low-income family, and he back then cultivated avocados. He had five brothers. Juan, Miguel, Marin, Abraham, and Marin. He dropped out of primary school when he was in fifth class, and he dropped out to work in the fields. Soon after some years, he decided to immigrate illegally to the US State of California in the 1980s. So he had to use different names back then like, “Miguel Valadez,” “Carlos Hernández Mendoza,” “Rubén Ávila,” and many more.

Soon he started stealing things and carried a loaded gun. But he was arrested at the age of 19, and after two months of his arrest, his first child was born. He was arrested again for selling the narcotics, and they got out.
